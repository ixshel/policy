Overview

GeoMock Location Changer is an Android application that allows users to simulate device GPS locations for development, testing, and debugging purposes.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we want you to understand exactly what data is and is not collected when you use GeoMock Location Changer.

Summary: GeoMock Location Changer does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information or location data to our servers. No account or login is required to use the app.

Information We Collect

GeoMock Location Changer is designed with your privacy in mind. Specifically:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to our servers.
  • We do not require you to create an account or log in.
  • We do not collect personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number).
  • We do not sell personal information.

The app may request the device's location permission solely to provide its core mock-location functionality. This permission is used on-device only and the data is never transmitted to us.

Third-party SDKs integrated into the app (see Third-Party Services) may independently collect certain non-personal technical information such as Advertising ID or device identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

How Information Is Used

Because GeoMock Location Changer does not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose. All mock-location processing occurs entirely on your device.

Any data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) is used by those services in accordance with their own privacy policies to serve contextually relevant advertisements and improve their products. We do not have access to or control over this data.

Third-Party Services

GeoMock Location Changer integrates the following third-party services. Each service operates under its own terms and privacy policy.

Google AdMob

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect information such as your Advertising ID and device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how this data is handled.

Google Play Billing

The app offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Remove Ads") through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment or financial information. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Google Play Services

The app uses Google Play Services for core Android functionality. Google Play Services may collect certain device and usage information. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Security

Because GeoMock Location Changer does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data at risk from our systems. All mock-location operations are performed locally on your device.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information that may be processed by third-party services, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to review the security practices of the third-party services listed above.

Children's Privacy

GeoMock Location Changer is intended for users aged 18 and older. The app is designed for developers, testers, and technically proficient users who need to simulate GPS locations.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately using the details in the Contact Us section so that we can take appropriate action.
