1. Information We Collect

Short answer: We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information or location data to our servers.

GeoMock Location Changer does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following to our servers:

  • Real or simulated GPS location data
  • Personal identification information
  • Account credentials (the App does not require account creation or login)
  • Device identifiers beyond what is described under Third-Party Services below

The App may request the ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION permissions solely to enable its core functionality of simulating GPS locations on your device. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to us.

Third-party services integrated into the App (described in Section 3) may independently collect certain technical information as governed by their own privacy policies.

2. How Information Is Used

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not use it in any way. Specifically:

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for profiling or targeted advertising on our end.
  • We do not share personal information with third parties, except as may be inherent to the operation of third-party SDKs described in Section 3.

Any data collected by third-party SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) is used by those third parties in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Because GeoMock does not collect or transmit personal data to our servers, the risk of a data breach on our end is minimised.

All on-device operations performed by the App remain on your device. We recommend keeping your Android operating system and the App updated to benefit from the latest security patches.

Please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your privacy, we cannot guarantee absolute security of information handled by third-party services.

5. Children's Privacy

GeoMock Location Changer is intended for users aged 18 and older. The App is designed for developers, testers, and technically proficient users who need GPS simulation capabilities.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App or its integrated third-party services, please contact us at the email address listed in Section 7 so we can take appropriate action.
